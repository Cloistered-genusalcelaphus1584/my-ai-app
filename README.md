1. Find the file you downloaded in your Downloads folder.

5. Locate the my-ai-app icon on your desktop and double-click it to open the program.

## 📚 Building your knowledge base

You create a library of your documents to get started.

1. Open the dashboard in the application.
2. Select the button labeled Add Documents.
3. Choose the files from your computer. You can upload text files, PDF documents, or Word documents.
4. Click the Start Processing button.
5. The software breaks your documents into small segments. It creates a map of these segments to make them searchable.
6. Wait for the progress bar to reach 100 percent. The software notifies you when the library is ready.

## 💡 Asking questions

Once you build your library, you can search your documents.

1. Click the Chat tab on the left menu.
2. Type your question into the text box at the bottom of the window.
3. Press the Enter key on your keyboard.
4. The system looks through your private library for information related to your question.
5. The assistant displays an answer based on your uploaded files. It also displays which documents provided the information.

## 🔒 Your data and privacy

This application processes data locally where possible. It uses your private documents to generate context for your questions. Your information does not become part of a public database. You maintain control over the documents you upload. You can delete documents from your library at any time through the Settings menu.

## 🔧 Managing your files

You can update your library as your information changes.

1. Go to the Management menu.
2. View a list of all current documents in your library.
3. Click the Delete icon next to any document to remove it from the searchable index.
4. Add new documents to keep your information current. The system automatically creates new search maps for any new content.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems, check these items first.

* App refuses to open: Check if another version is already running in your system tray. Close the existing version and try again.
* Files do not process: Ensure your documents contain readable text and are not images or scans.
* App feels slow: Close other programs that consume heavy memory. A knowledge base with many files requires more computer resources.
* Update notifications: When a new version releases, the app notifies you. Download and run the installer again to perform an upgrade. Your existing documents remain safe during this update.

## 📋 Frequently asked questions

Does this app require a subscription? No. You use the software as a standalone tool on your own machine.

Can I search multiple files at once? Yes. The system searches across your entire uploaded collection every time you ask a question.

What file types work best? Plain text and standard PDFs work most effectively. Avoid complex files with many images or tables.

Does the app work without internet? The system requires an internet connection to send your questions to the processing engine. It keeps your files local.
